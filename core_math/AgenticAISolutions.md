# AgenticAISolutions

**Superclass:** [[MarketCompetency]]

Agentic AI solutions are built on linear algebra for vector embeddings and similarity search (HNSW), and probabilistic systems for next-token prediction, Bayesian reasoning, and decision-making under uncertainty.

## Underpinned By

- [[LinearAlgebra]] — Vector embeddings, SVD, similarity search in vector indexes
- [[ProbabilisticSystems]] — LLM next-token prediction, Bayesian inference, MDP-based planning
