# Bayes' Theorem

**Part of:** [[ProbabilisticSystems]]

Bayes' theorem describes the probability of an event given prior evidence: P(A|B) = P(B|A)·P(A) / P(B). It is the mathematical foundation for Bayesian inference, belief updating, and probabilistic reasoning.

## Applications in Agentic AI

- LLM next-token prediction (autoregressive conditional probability)
- Bayesian optimization for hyperparameter tuning
- Uncertainty estimation in agent decisions

## Applications in Multi-Cloud

- Anomaly detection with Bayesian changepoint models
- Predictive autoscaling based on probabilistic demand forecasts
- A/B testing and statistical inference for deployment decisions
